Warren House 🏡

According to one of the writers of Annabelle 3: Homecoming (2019), James Wan, the film takes place in the Warren house, which was where Ed (Patrick Wilson) and Lorraine (Vera Farmiga) took the Annabelle doll, besides being the place where all the other objects are.
The Real Dol🎎

The real Annabelle doll is located in Connecticut, United States. It is kept in a display case protected by glass and attention notices at the Warren Museum of Occultism, which the couple themselves founded.
Prequel and Sequel 📽

This will be a prequel and also a continuation of the films in The Conjuring Universe franchise.
Where is the Dol 🤷‍♀️

Before starring in her own film, the Annabelle doll — created by James Wan based on a real artifact that is part of the Occult Museum of the Warrens' collection — was a hit in Wan's The Conjuring, despite only appearing in the prologue of the film. movie
Cast Change 💃

In Annabelle 3: Homecoming (2019), actress McKenna Grace plays the character Judy Warren. In previous films, the role was played by actress Sterling Jerins.
Completing the Franchise 👯

This will be the seventh film in The Conjuring Universe and the third in the Annabelle franchise.
